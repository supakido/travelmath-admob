Privacy Policy for Travel Math

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Travel Math ("we," "our," or "us") collects and processes:

  • Currency exchange rate data
  • Local price data for cities
  • Travel budget calculations
  • User-inputted expense data (stored locally only)
  • Ad-related identifiers for advertising purposes

2. How We Use Information

The app uses data to:

  • Display real-time exchange rates
  • Calculate currency conversions
  • Provide travel budget recommendations
  • Track travel expenses
  • Show relevant advertisements

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any external databases of user information. Your travel expenses and budget data remain private on your device.

4. Advertisements

We use Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Usage data
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Reset app preferences
  • Clear expense history
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Please review it periodically.
